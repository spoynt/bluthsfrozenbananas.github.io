About Us

Our Family

Nestled along the bustling boardwalk, our stand has been a beloved fixture in the seaside community, serving up smiles and frozen delights for generations. Established by the visionary patriarch George Bluth Sr., our stand embodies the spirit of entrepreneurial excellence and family tradition. Led by eldest son Michael, we continue to uphold the legacy of quality and innovation that has made us a beloved institution. Join us as we invite you to experience the joy and nostalgia of Bluth's Original Frozen Banana Stand, where every bite is a taste of family history and seaside charm.
